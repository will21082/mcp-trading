## 🎓 Understanding the Breakout Strategy

### What is a Bollinger Band Squeeze?

A BB Squeeze occurs when:
1. **BB Width < 0.03** - The bands are narrow (low volatility)
2. **Price consolidates** - Trading in tight range
3. **Pressure builds** - Like a spring being compressed
4. **Breakout follows** - Energy releases in strong move

### The Scoring System Explained

Our scanner uses a **10-point scoring system**:

| Criteria | Points | Why It Matters |
|----------|--------|----------------|
| BB Squeeze (BBW < 0.03) | +2 | Identifies compression before breakout |
| Bullish Breakout (Price > Upper BB + 2%) | +3 | Confirms upward momentum |
| Oversold Bounce (Price < Lower BB, RSI < 30) | +2 | Reversal opportunity |
| RSI Healthy (30-70) | +1 | Avoids overbought/oversold extremes |
| Volume Spike (>1.5x avg) | +1 | Confirms genuine interest |
| Strong Trend (ADX > 25) | +1 | Trend is tradeable |
| BB Rating +2 or higher | +2 | Multiple BB confirmations |
| BB Rating +1 | +1 | Moderate BB signal |

**Minimum Score:** 5/10 to generate signal
**Recommended Entry:** 6/10 or higher

### Technical Indicators

**Bollinger Bands (20, 2):**
- Middle: 20-period SMA
- Upper: Middle + (2 × Standard Deviation)
- Lower: Middle - (2 × Standard Deviation)
- Width: (Upper - Lower) / Middle

**RSI (14):**
- Relative Strength Index
- Overbought: > 70
- Oversold: < 30
- Healthy: 30-70

**ADX (14):**
- Average Directional Index
- Strong trend: > 25
- Weak trend: < 20

**MACD (12, 26, 9):**
- Bullish: MACD > 0
- Bearish: MACD < 0

def analyze_signal(data):

    # 1. Check BB Squeeze
    if bbw < 0.03:
        score += 2

    # 2. Check Breakout

        score += 3
    elif price < lower_bb and rsi < 30:

        score += 1
    elif rsi > 70:
        score -= 1  # Penalty for overbought

    # 4. Volume Confirmation
    if volume > 1.5 × avg_volume:
